Creative Campaign

MEA Cultural Localization System

Challenge

创造性素材在穆斯林市场容易触碰文化禁忌。Find X8 前期测试中，party 场景被标记 —— 在部分中东非市场，这类场景可能引发文化冲突。手动审核每个市场的每张素材既不具扩展性，也不可持续。

Action

建立了一套跨文化创意预审流程：在创意 brief 阶段就嵌入文化合规检查点；与区域市场团队共建禁忌场景清单；将 party / 夜间社交场景替换为家庭欢聚 / 夜间旅行等普适场景。整个流程在 2 周内完成，并沉淀为可复用的 SOP。

Content Strategy

Short-Form Video Transformation

Challenge

Previous campaign content relied heavily on feature-demonstration long videos (60s+). Completion rates were declining quarter over quarter, especially among the Reno target demographic (Gen Z). The content format was misaligned with platform behavior.

Action

Led the pivot to platform-native short-form series. Instead of one hero video, we produced 6–8 scenario-based clips per product (travel, low-light, portrait) optimized for TikTok/Instagram Reels/Shorts algorithms. Partnered with local creators in Thailand and Indonesia to add authentic local flavor.

Result

↑ 45%

About Me
I believe good marketing starts with listening.

I started my career as a TV producer and director at Shenzhen Media Group, where I learned the fundamentals of storytelling and audience attention. That foundation carried me through creative agencies serving top gaming clients, to ByteDance's gaming division, and finally to OPPO — where I've spent 4 years leading IMC for key product lines across 13 global markets.

Along the way, I earned an MSc in Global Marketing from the University of Sheffield and a dual BA from Shenzhen University. I thrive at the intersection of creative intuition and data-driven decision-making — knowing when a campaign needs more emotion, and when it needs more evidence.

I'm a naturally curious person who loves exploring new things. These days I'm teaching myself AI tools and workflows — because the next generation of marketing will be built at the intersection of human creativity and machine intelligence. Currently based in Shenzhen. Open to global opportunities.
